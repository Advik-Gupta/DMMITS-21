NBA 2K21 is a basketball simulation video game that was developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports, based on the National Basketball Association (NBA). It is the 22nd installment in the NBA 2K franchise and the successor to NBA 2K20, and the predecessor to NBA 2K22. The game was released on September 4,2020 for Microsoft Windows, Nintendo Switch, GameStation 4, Xbox One, and Stadia, and on November 12, 2020 for GameStation 5 and Xbox Series X and Series S.
